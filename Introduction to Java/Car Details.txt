Car Details


Write a program to get the details about a car as specified in the sample input and display it. 

Hint: Store the price in double. Print the price correct to two decimal places.


Sample Input 1:
Enter the car name:
amaze
Enter the car no:
3666
Enter the price:
870000.76

Sample Output 1:

Carname:amaze
Carno:3666
Price:870000.76 rs only
